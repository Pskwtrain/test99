Blue Gushers



Blue Gushers is an indica-dominant strain that boasts a unique flavor and potent effects. It gets its name from its colorful appearance and sweet, fruity taste, which is reminiscent of gusher candy. The buds of this strain are dense, covered in trichomes, and feature a deep blue color with purple hues.

Pancake


Pancakes is a unique and delicious strain that is sure to become a favorite among cannabis enthusiasts. It gets its name from its creamy, syrup-like flavor, which is reminiscent of warm, fluffy pancakes. Pancakes is an indica-dominant strain that is known for its relaxing and euphoric effects.

MAC 1


Mac 1, also known as the Miracle Alien Cookies X1 strain, is a potent strain with mysterious origins. It’s been backcrossed behind many Mac strains to produce an offspring that is simply legendary. This particular strain is grown organically from our producers so that you can taste the beauty of it’s genetics.

Lemon Skunk


If the zesty citrus flavour of Lemon Skunk doesn’t draw you in, then the happy, energetic head buzz surely will! This award-winning sativa-dominant hybrid stimulates the mind and relaxes the body. If you’re looking for a strain to help channel focus and creativity, Lemon Skunk is for you!





All Time High



All Time High, a meticulously balanced hybrid that’s the epitome of cannabis excellence. Collins Ave, in collaboration with Cookies and Kaia Kush, created All Time High as a genetic masterpiece from OG Kush and Strawberry Diesel. It boasts a perfectly even split of 50% sativa and 50% indica, aiming to elevate your cannabis experience to new heights.


Purple Space Cookies


Purple buds, star-like trichomes shining in the light and hints of pine – is this heaven? It may not be, but it’s the closest thing we’ve found. Your plans won’t be railroaded when smoking too much, but you will be left bleary-eyed and cheesing a big grin. Let’s say you won’t be stuck to the couch, but you’ll soon forget your back was ever sore. It’s guaranteed to place a smile on your face. If you like good vibes and an unwavering pep in your step, you’ll love this strain.
